Homemade Gatorade Recipie


- One slice of bread.
- One application of (preferably chunky for this recipe) peanute butter. If your alergic to eanut butter you may want to check out this article: http://greatist.com/health/healthy-alternatives-peanut-butter and you'll find you have many nice alternatives like Hazelnut, Pecan, Almond and even Coconut.
- A couple pinches of cinnamon powder.
- A could pinch/sprinkles of cocoa nibs
- One glass of soy milk!


That's it! The cocoa nibs have a interesting union with the cinnamon powerder taste and the nibs add a extra crunch as well!
